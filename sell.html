How to write a great pet description

Include personality traits, energy level, health status, vaccination records, and any special needs. Mention if they're good with children, other pets, or specific behaviors. Be honest and detailed to attract the right buyers.

Tips for taking great pet photos

Use natural lighting, get down to the pet's eye level, capture their personality, and show multiple angles. Include photos of the pet playing, resting, and interacting. Clean backgrounds work best. Take at least 5-10 photos to choose from.

What information should I collect from buyers?

Request their living situation (house/apartment), experience with pets, other pets in home, presence of children, and their schedule. Ask about veterinary plans and if they've researched the breed's needs. This helps ensure a good match.

How to price your pets fairly
